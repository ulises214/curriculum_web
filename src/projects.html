Walle's Weather App

An application that displays weather information by consuming the OpenWeatherMap api. For this application I based on several resources but all of them were modified by me to adapt it to the design that I like, these changes were from the layout, colors, icons theme and customization options.

Some features are:

  • Weather in current time, next hours and next days.
  • Sunrise and sunset time
  • City search in different languages.
  • Current weather in the cities found.
  • Change theme [Dark / Light].
  • Change units of measurement [Metric / Imperial].
  • Change of language [English / Spanish].
